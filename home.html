WELCOME

"Pancake Recipe"

Ingredients:

1 large egg

1 can Alaska Sweetened Condensed Milk

1/4 tsp vanilla or pandan extract

1 cup fresh milk

1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

2 tsps baking powder

1/4 cup of butter

"Procedure"

1. Mix eggs, Alaska Sweetened Condensed Milk, vanilla or pandan extract and water.

2. In another bowl, combine flour and baking powder. Pour in egg-micture into the dry ingedridents and mix until batter is just a bit lumpy.

3. Heat and add about a tablespoon of butter to a non stick pan. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter and cook for about a minute or until small bubbles start to pop to top. Flip the cook for another 30 seconds. Repeat until batter is finished.

4. Drizzle pancakes with remaining condensed milk. Serve hot
